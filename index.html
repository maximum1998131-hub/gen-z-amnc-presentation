07 / 08

Intergenerational Learning

Reverse Mentorship Reduces
Information Asymmetry

Young people bring

  • frontier exposure
  • hands-on experimentation
  • workflow-level signals
  • early signs of quality risks
  • practical AI use cases

Reverse mentorship connects the two.

Senior leaders bring

  • judgment
  • context
  • governance
  • business priorities
  • decision authority

Reverse mentorship is not about young people teaching senior leaders tricks. It is about helping organizations reduce information asymmetry in a fast-moving technology transition.
